# Qur'an Error Corrector - Quran Memorization FSRS | مصحح أخطاء القرآن

**Qur'an Error Corrector** is an Anki add-on for one very specific moment: you have just finished your normal Qur'an revision, you noticed a mistake, and you want Anki/FSRS to help you repair that exact weak spot without turning the whole mushaf into a giant deck.

You keep your normal revision load outside Anki. The add-on is only for the half-pages and passages where mistakes actually happened.

![Qur'an Error Corrector overview](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/cover-new.png)

## The Workflow

1. Revise normally outside Anki.
2. Open **Tools -> Qur'an Error Corrector**.
3. Go to the Madani-style page where the mistake happened.
4. Select the exact word or passage.
5. Choose the mistake type, difficulty, and highlight color.
6. Create the card.
7. Let Anki/FSRS bring that correction back at the right time.

![Selecting a Qur'an mistake and creating a card](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/select-error-create-card.gif)

## What The Card Contains

- **Front:** the closest useful half-page context before the mistake.
- **Back:** the full page with the mistake highlighted.
- **Anchoring help:** a short correction routine adapted to the difficulty.
- **Automatic tags:** surah, mistake type, and difficulty.
- **Custom highlight color:** choose the color that makes the error easiest to spot.

![Front and back card anatomy](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/card-anatomy-new.png)

## Built For Qur'an Revision

Qur'an revision is sequential. A mistake rarely lives as an isolated word floating outside the page. This add-on keeps the page as the mental landmark, but uses half-page context to make the prompt focused enough for spaced repetition.

It also avoids cutting the prompt at arbitrary places. Half-page boundaries are adjusted to clear stopping points or verse endings whenever possible, so the card feels natural to recite.

![Correction workflow](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/workflow.png)

## Using Anki On iPhone?

You use Anki on your iPhone and you are frustrated that mobile Anki cannot run add-ons? No panic: I made exactly what you need.

Use the mobile CSV companion site:

https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/mobile/

It gives you almost the same workflow as the add-on: select the error on the mushaf page, choose the type and difficulty, preview the front/back, then export everything as a two-column CSV file.

On iPhone, download the CSV, tap **Open with -> Anki**, and import your errors. During import, make sure the fields match correctly: **Front** with **Front**, **Back** with **Back**, and leave **Tags** empty.

## Review Experience

During review, the card asks you to recite the context first, then continue into the passage that caused the mistake. The answer shows the full page, with the selected error highlighted clearly.

![Reviewing a generated correction card in Anki](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/review-generated-card.gif)

## Requirements

- Designed for Anki 25.x.
- Works best with FSRS enabled in your deck options.
- Uses QCF V2 page fonts for the rendered mushaf pages.
- The add-on creates targeted Qur'an memorization correction cards; Anki remains responsible for scheduling.

## Good Fit If...

You already have a Qur'an revision routine, such as revising a juz' per day, and you only want spaced repetition for the half-pages or passages where mistakes actually happen.
