# Qur'an Error Corrector **Qur'an Error Corrector** is an Anki add-on for one very specific moment: you have just finished your normal Qur'an revision, you noticed a mistake, and you want Anki/FSRS to help you repair that exact weak spot without taking over your whole revision routine. It is not meant to turn the whole mushaf into a giant deck. You keep your normal revision load outside Anki, then use Anki only for the half-pages and passages that actually caused trouble. ![Qur'an Error Corrector overview](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/cover.png) ## The Workflow 1. Revise normally outside Anki. 2. Open **Tools -> Qur'an Error Corrector**. 3. Go to the Madani-style page where the mistake happened. 4. Select the exact word or passage. 5. Choose the mistake type and difficulty. 6. Create the card. 7. Let Anki/FSRS bring that correction back at the right time. ![Selecting a Qur'an mistake and creating a card](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/select-error-create-card.gif) ## What The Card Contains - **Front:** the closest useful half-page context before the mistake. - **Back:** the full page with the mistake highlighted. - **Anchoring help:** a short correction routine adapted to the difficulty. - **Automatic tags:** surah, mistake type, and difficulty. ![Front and back card anatomy](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/card-anatomy.png) ## Built For Qur'an Revision Qur'an revision is sequential. A mistake rarely lives as an isolated word floating outside the page. The add-on keeps the page as the mental landmark, but uses half-page context to make the prompt focused enough for spaced repetition. It also avoids cutting the prompt at arbitrary places. Half-page boundaries are adjusted to clear stopping points or verse endings whenever possible, so the card feels natural to recite. ![Correction workflow](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/workflow.png) ## Languages The interface and generated cards are available in multiple languages: - French - Arabic - English - Spanish - Turkish - Indonesian Arabic uses right-to-left layout automatically. ## Review Experience During review, the card asks you to recite the context first, then continue into the passage that caused the mistake. The answer shows the full page, with the selected error highlighted clearly. ![Reviewing a generated correction card in Anki](https://bjr33127-hub.github.io/quran-error-corrector-addon-page/assets/review-generated-card.gif) ## Requirements - Designed for Anki 25.x. - Works best with FSRS enabled in your deck options. - Uses QCF V2 page fonts for the rendered mushaf pages. - The add-on creates targeted correction cards; Anki remains responsible for scheduling. ## Good Fit If... You already have a Qur'an revision routine, such as revising a juz' per day, and you only want spaced repetition for the half-pages or passages where mistakes actually happen.
