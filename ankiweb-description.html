Qur'an Error Corrector

Qur'an Error Corrector is an Anki add-on for one very specific moment: you have just finished your normal Qur'an revision, you noticed a mistake, and you want Anki/FSRS to help you repair that exact weak spot without taking over your whole revision routine.

Instead of turning every page into a flashcard, the add-on lets you open a Madani-style mushaf page, select the word or passage where the mistake happened, and create a targeted correction card.

Visual preview, screenshots, and GIF demos: open the project page.

How it works

  1. Revise normally outside Anki.
  2. Open the add-on from Anki's Tools menu.
  3. Select the word or passage where you made a mistake.
  4. Create or update the card.
  5. Let Anki/FSRS bring that correction back at the right time.

Demo GIF: selecting a Qur'an mistake and creating a card.

What the card contains

  • Front: the closest useful half-page context before the mistake.
  • Back: the full page with the mistake highlighted.
  • Anchoring help: a short correction routine based on the difficulty you chose.
  • Automatic tags: surah, mistake type, and difficulty.

Card preview: front and back card anatomy.

Built for Qur'an revision

The add-on does not pretend that Qur'an review is a pile of isolated snippets. It keeps the page as the mental landmark, but uses half-page context when creating the prompt. It also avoids splitting the prompt at arbitrary places: half-page boundaries are adjusted to clear stopping points or verse endings whenever possible.

Workflow image: how the correction loop works.

Languages

The interface and generated cards are available in multiple languages: French, Arabic, English, plus Spanish, Turkish, and Indonesian. Arabic uses right-to-left layout automatically.

Requirements and notes

  • Designed for Anki 25.x.
  • Works best with FSRS enabled in your deck options.
  • The add-on creates targeted correction cards; Anki remains responsible for scheduling.
  • Uses QCF V2 page fonts for the rendered mushaf pages.

Review GIF: reviewing a generated correction card in Anki.

Good fit if...

You already have a Qur'an revision routine, such as revising a juz' per day, and you only want spaced repetition for the half-pages or passages where mistakes actually happen.
